About

This space is a collaborative community project; made by the people, for the people.

Our Mission

is to bring together passionate people to collaborate and to build an online dictionary for indigenous language in Borneo.

Our Vision

is to produce digital materials on indigenous language accessable to everyone anywhere in the world.

Volunteer Opportunities

Our volunteering programme is open to anyone regardless of race and background. You don't have to know any indigenous Sabahan language to be part of this project.

Language Moderators

We are looking for language moderators who are able to monitor, edit and ensure that the vocabulary meaning is correct. Volunteers must be able to speak the language fluently to become a moderator.

Village Trips

We organize regular weekend trips to villages around Sabah for word hunting while spending time to learn not only the language, but also the true Sabah indigenous culture.
