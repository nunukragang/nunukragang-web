Nunukragang Project

A community-based organisation dedicated to empowering people to collaborate and build their own native language dictionary.

Learn More

About

This space is a collaborative community project; made by the people, for the people.

Our Mission

To bring together passionate people to collaborate and to build an online dictionary for indigenous language in Borneo.

Our Vision

To produce digital materials on indigenous language accessible to everyone anywhere in the world.

What We Do

We develop an online dictionary platform accessible to everyone, anywhere and anytime.

Online Dictionary

Users can easily use the dictionary that contains vocabulary from different languages to different translations, all contributed by native speakers.

Volunteer Opportunities

Our volunteering programme is open to anyone regardless of race and background. Fluency in any indigenous Sabahan language is not mandatory to be part of this programme.

Language Moderators

We are looking for language moderators who are able to monitor, edit and ensure that the vocabulary meaning is correct. Volunteers must be able to speak the language fluently to become a moderator.

Village Trips

We organize regular weekend trips to villages around Sabah for word hunting while spending time to learn not only the language, but also the true Sabah indigenous culture.
