Build and Preserve Indigenous Language Together

Nunukragang is a community-powered initiative creating accessible digital resources for indigenous languages in Borneo, including a living online dictionary for learners, speakers, and future generations.

About Nunukragang

This project is made by the community, for the community.

Our Mission

Bring together passionate people to collaboratively build an online dictionary for indigenous languages in Borneo.

Our Vision

Create inclusive digital language resources accessible to everyone, everywhere, while celebrating cultural identity.

What We Do

We design practical, community-driven tools to support language preservation.

Online Dictionary Platform

Access an evolving bilingual dictionary contributed to and moderated by native speakers for clarity, relevance, and authenticity.

Community Contributions

Members contribute definitions, translations, and cultural context, helping preserve language nuances that matter.

Multiple Bahasa & Dialect Options

Explore dialects including Lotud and Kimaragang to better understand linguistic diversity and local identity.

Volunteer Opportunities

Open to everyone regardless of race and background. Native language fluency is welcome but not always required.

Language Moderators

Help monitor and refine entries to keep dictionary meanings accurate and trustworthy for the public.

Village Trips

Join weekend field trips around Sabah to gather words, stories, and cultural context directly from local communities.
