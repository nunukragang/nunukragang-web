Sponsor Nunukragang Project

Your sponsorship supports indigenous language documentation and keeps the work sustainable.

What is this sponsorship for?

Nunukragang sponsorship allows individuals and organizations to fund dictionary word entries for endangered and under-documented indigenous languages in Borneo.

Sponsored funds are used to support ongoing work such as data collection, translation, review, validation, and dictionary publishing.

How we plan to use sponsorship funds

  • Provide fair compensation for volunteers who contribute verified word entries.
  • Pay moderators who review spelling, meaning, and cultural context for each entry.
  • Cover operational costs for maintaining the dictionary platform and updates.

Sponsor names can be displayed on sponsored words as recognition of your contribution.

We are committed to transparency. Full sponsorship usage details and breakdowns are available on the sponsorship page once you log in to Nunukragang.
